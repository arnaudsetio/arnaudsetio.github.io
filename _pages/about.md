Arnaud Arindra Adiyoso Setio is a research scientist at [Siemens Healthineers](https://www.healthcare.siemens.com/). He was working as a PhD student at the [Diagnostic Image Analysis Group](http://diagnijmegen.nl/index.php/Home) on the automatic lung nodules detection and classification in chest CT, supervised by [Bram van Ginneken](http://diagnijmegen.nl/index.php/Person?name=Bram_van_Ginneken). He studied Electrical Engineering for his bachelor study in [Institut Teknologi Bandung](http://www.itb.ac.id/en/), and for his master study at [Eindhoven University of Technology](https://www.tue.nl/).

His research interests include deep learning, medical image analysis, computer vision, and machine learning.

During his free time, he is actively involved in music ensembles, mostly orchestra, and angklung. He was a conductor of Nusantara Student Ensemble (string) and [Angklung Eindhoven](http://www.angklungeindhoven.com/).

Research interest: deep learning, computer vision, video processing, image processing, digital electronic design, computer architecture
